## 从BaseWindow中临时注释的段落
这一段是用于显示日志的，后来实现了日志详情的界面，因此该功能就不需要了

```xml
<TabItem
    Height="30"
    FontSize="16"
    Header="日志显示">
    <ListView
        x:Name="LogListView"
        BorderBrush="Transparent"
        FontFamily="微软雅黑">
        <ListView.ContextMenu>
            <ContextMenu>
                <MenuItem Header="清空日志" />
            </ContextMenu>
        </ListView.ContextMenu>
        <ListView.ItemTemplate>
            <DataTemplate>
                <StackPanel>
                    <TextBlock
                        FontSize="10"
                        Foreground="{Binding Path=LogLevel, Converter={StaticResource LogLevelConverter}}"
                        Text="{Binding Path=LogTime, Converter={StaticResource DateTimeConverter}}" />
                    <TextBlock
                        FontSize="16"
                        Foreground="{Binding Path=LogLevel, Converter={StaticResource LogLevelConverter}}"
                        Text="{Binding Path=LogMessage}" />
                </StackPanel>
            </DataTemplate>
        </ListView.ItemTemplate>
    </ListView>
</TabItem>
<TabItem
    Height="30"
    FontSize="16"
    Header="数据显示" />
```
